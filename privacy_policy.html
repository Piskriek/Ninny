1. Information We Collect

Little Bloom Academy does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information. All data created while using the App (progress records, star counts, guardian notes, and activity logs) is stored exclusively on your device using your browser's localStorage. This data never leaves your device and is never sent to any external server.

2. Children's Privacy

We take children's privacy extremely seriously. The App is designed for use by young children alongside a parent or guardian. We do not:

  • Collect names, photos, or any personally identifiable information from children
  • Display third-party advertising
  • Track usage analytics or behaviour
  • Require an account, login, or registration
  • Connect to the internet during normal gameplay (the App works offline)

3. Data Storage

Progress data (stars earned, activities completed, and caretaker notes) is saved locally on your device using the browser's built-in localStorage. This data can be deleted at any time by clearing your browser/app cache, or by using the built-in "Reset Progress" button in the Guardian Progress dashboard.

4. Premium Features

The App offers optional premium content ("Extended Play"). The unlocked premium status is stored locally on your device. No financial transaction data is processed by the App itself. Any purchases are handled securely through the Google Play Store billing system.

5. Permissions

The App requests no special device permissions (no camera, microphone, location, or contacts access is required or requested).

6. Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

7. Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last Updated" date above. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
